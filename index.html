About

I graduated from UCSB with a B.S. in Biopsychology and aim to combine my neuroscience background with web developing skills I acquired through the Coding Bootcamp at UCSD Extension. I aspire to do research that involves creating neural simulations in order to better predict various pharmacological outcomes in the brain. I am particularly interested in mental disorders and abolishing the stigmas around them via scientific evidence.

I enjoy exercising, being outdoors, and playing with my pup, Mika. It is the best when I can do all three simultaneously! I love hiking with her, as well as taking her to Dog Beach to run and swim together. She is my number one adventure buddy!
